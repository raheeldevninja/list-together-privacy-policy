Information Collected while Using the Application

We collect information to provide features of our Application, such as your full name, email address, password, and profile picture. This information is securely stored using Firebase servers.

The collection of your full name, email address, password, and profile picture is mandatory for the sign-up process in the List Together app. These details are required to create and manage your account and are used within the app, such as displaying your full name and profile picture. As these data elements are essential for using the app, users cannot disable the collection of this information.
